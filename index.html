About BWA Calendar

Note: This calendar is created and maintained by a parent, not the school. It attempts to aggregate all school communications and emails to automatically centralize events here for convenience.

The system uses the School Calendar Aggregator to parse email forwards and attachments (PDF/Docx), identifying event dates, student streams, and important links.

Please always cross-reference with official school announcements.
